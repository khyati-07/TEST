 work only only on SS and FRU parameter.

 For TLR_SS (20 Marks)
SS = f (NT, NE) × 15 + f(NP) × 5
The functions f (NT, NE) and f(Np) are determined
by NIRF.
NT represents the total sanctioned approved intake
in the institution, including all undergraduate (UG)
and postgraduate (PG) programs.


For TLR_FRU (30 Marks)
FRU = 7.5 × f(BC) + 22.5 × f(BO)
BC represents the average annual capital
expenditure per student for the past three years,
specifically related to the engineering discipline.
This excludes any expenditure on the construction
of new buildings. 
BO represents the average annual operational (or
recurring) expenditure per student for the previous
three years, pertaining to the engineering discipline
only. This excludes expenses related to the
maintenance of hostels and allied services.
